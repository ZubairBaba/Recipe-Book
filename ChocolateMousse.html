CHOCOLATE MOUSE

Chocolate Mouse recipe
INGREDIENTS AMOUNT
Eggs 3
Dark Chocolate 125 grams
Unsalted Butter 10 grams
Cream( full fat ) 1/2 Cup
Castor Sugar 3 Tbsp
-------------------------------- ------------------------------------------------------
Total Ingredients 5

METHOD:

* Separate eggs

* Whisk yolks

* Melt chocolate and butter

* Whip cream till you have stiff peaks

* Whip egg whites and slowly add sugar

* fold egg yolks into cream( 8 folds max )

* Chocolate should be runny and luke warm

* Add chocolate to cream yolk mixture( 8 folds max )

* Add 1/4 egg white into chocolate and combine

* Pour the chocolate into the egg white

* Decorate and ENJOY!
