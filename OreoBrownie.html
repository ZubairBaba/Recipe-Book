OREO BROWNIE

Oreo Brownie recipe
INGREDIENTS AMOUNT
Eggs 4
Sugar 2 Cups
Oil 1 Cup
Water 1/4 Cup
Cocoa 2 Cups
Baking Powder 1 tsp
Cake Flour 1 Cup
Strong Coffee 3 Tbsp
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Oreo 30
Total Ingredients 10

INSTRUCTIONS:

* Separate oreo's and remove the icing in the middle

* lightly crush the biscuit and set aside

* Whisk together the eggs, sugar, oil, oreo icing, coffee and water until combined

* Add the cocoa, baking powder, cake flour and crushed oreo

* Pour into a non-stick baking tray

* Bake at 180 degrees for 20-25 min or until skewer comes out dry

* Cut immediately into desired size

* Add toppings of your choice or enjoy with ice-cream
