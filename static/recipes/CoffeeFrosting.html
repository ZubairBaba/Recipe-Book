COFFEE FROSTING

Coffee Frosting recipe
INGREDIENTS AMOUNT
Butter, Softened 3/4 Cup
Cream Cheese, Softened 1/2 Cups
Icing Sugar 2 Cup
Cocoa 1/4 Cup
Strong Brewed Coffee 3 Tbsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
-------------------------------- ------------------------------------------------------
Total Ingredients 6

METHOD:

* Mix butter & cream cheese until creamy

* Add the rest of the ingredients and whip until smooth

* Let it rest in the fridge for 30min or until stiff

* ENJOY!
