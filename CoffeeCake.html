COFFEE CAKE

Coffee cake recipe
INGREDIENTS AMOUNT
All-Purpose Flour 1 & 3/4 Cups
Sugar 2 Cups
Cocoa 3/4 Cup
Baking Powder 1 tsp
Baking Soda 2 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Large Eggs 2
Buttermilk 1 Cup
Strong Coffee 1 Cup
Oil 1/2 Cup
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Total Ingredients 11

INSTRUCTIONS:

* Whisk together dry mixture

* Whisk together wet mixture

* Pour into 2 round baking-pans

* Bake at 180 degrees for 35-40 min

* Allow cake to cool for 10-15 min

* Add frosting of your choice( preferably a coffee frosting )
